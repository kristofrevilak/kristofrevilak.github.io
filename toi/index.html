consumer psychology

Workshop is about deeply understanding consumer’s psychology, how they make decision and how to influence it.

Research shows, that up to 95% of purchasing decisions are subconscious and studies completed by neuroscience have found that people whose brains are damaged in the area that generates emotions are incapable of making decisions. So maybe after all, we are not as logical as we might think.

Behavioral science gives us the fundamental principles and tools for understanding both human consciousness and subconsciousness and how both influence what people buy.
